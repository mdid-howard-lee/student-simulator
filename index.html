▶ FOUR MECHANICS

That'll hit different.

No skill trees. No XP. Just four systems doing the quiet, brutal math of being fifteen — and asking you to live with the answer.

⏱CORE LOOP

Time is a currency.

Spend it. Lose it. Never get it back. Every action draws from the same wallet, and there's no overdraft.

◷WORLD

Your week is the map.

The school day is the dungeon. Bells are doors. Lunch is a save room. Friday is a boss fight you didn't see coming.

♥STATS

Pressure is a meter.

You don't lose when it breaks — you change. Different ending. Different version of you. We keep both saves.

⚖CONSEQUENCE

Choices stick.

Skip a club to study? The friend who invited you won't ask next week. The game remembers. That's the point.

Five choices. One you.

A few honest answers.
Q.
Is it sad?

Sometimes. But it's also the kind of game where a small win at lunch feels like beating a boss. So.

Q.
Do I have to be in school?

No. If you've ever been — or ever loved someone who is — you already speak the language.

Q.
Is there a "win"?

There are seventeen endings. None of them are perfect. We think that's the most honest thing about it.

Q.
Why are you making this?

Because nobody made it for me when I needed it. So I'm making it for whoever needs it next.
